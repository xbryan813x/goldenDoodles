New Parent Puppy Care and Registering

Grooming

I have been clipping the puppies nails and they will have already recieved their first grooming session by the rehoming date. After your first month with your Doodle remember to schedule their first grooming appointment to keep their coat in shape and to get the puppy used to the grooming process. This breed will require regular grooming about every 6-8 weeks with short lengths and 4-6 weeks for longer cuts.




Feeding

Dogs, especially growing puppies should be fed a high protein dog food. The easiest way to narrow out the low quality foods is to look at the ingredient list. The first ingredient should always be a protein. If you see the word "meal" at the end of your first ingredient, it is not a good pick! (ex: chicken meal, lamb meal, etc.)


Veterinary Care

Puppies will go home dewormed and with first set of shots given at 6 weeks of age and registration paperwork. Please remember to schedule the second round of shots (10 Weeks) with your vet or Kempsville Animal Hospital (Dam and litters vet durring whelping).

Registering Your Pup

Puppies are registered with the CKC and will go home with registration and pedigree paperwork. Owner records will need to be updated by the new family.
