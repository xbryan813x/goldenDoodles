Adoption Process

Step One

Reserve your puppy by filling out the New Family Information form and submitting your $500.00 deposit.

Step Two

Be part of your new furry friends growth by visiting our website weekly for updated pics and videos. The website will be updated every Sunday night.

Step Three

Puppies go home at 9 weeks of age (June 14th). Selections should have been completed at the time of your deposit. We will schedule appointments at 60 minute intervals in the oder of deposit. You will be informed well in advance of the dates and times so please schedule accordingly.

Step Four

If you live in the City of Virginia Beach, please register your puppy with the city.

Step Five

Puppies will go home with first set of shots given at 6 weeks of age and registration paperwork. Please remember to schedule the second round of shots (10 Weeks) with your vet or Kempsville Animal Hospital (Dam and litters vet durring whelping).

Step Six

Puppies are registered with the CKC, please update the owner records with your information.

Step Seven

I have been clipping the puppies nails and they will have already recieved their first grooming session by the rehoming date. After your first month with your Doodle remember to schedule their first grooming appointment to keep their coat in shape and mainly to get the puppy used to the grooming process.
