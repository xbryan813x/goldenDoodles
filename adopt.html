Adoption Process

Step One


Reserve your puppy by filling out the New Family Information form and submitting your $500.00 deposit or paying the amount in full.

Step Two


Be part of your new furry friends growth by visiting our website weekly for updated pics and videos. The website will be updated every Sunday night.

Step Three


Puppies go home at 9 weeks of age (June 14th). Selections should have been completed at the time of your deposit. We will schedule appointments at 60 minute intervals in the oder of deposit. You will be informed well in advance of the dates and times so please schedule accordingly.
